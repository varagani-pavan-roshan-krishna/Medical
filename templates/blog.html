Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

Developed by Varagani Pavan Roshan

As a Full Stack Developer and AI Enthusiast, I am passionate about building innovative solutions that make a difference. This project aims to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology by providing a machine-learning-powered web app for disease diagnosis.

The Problem

Many individuals experience symptoms but lack immediate access to medical consultations. Our solution empowers users with preliminary insights into their health concerns.

The Solution

Using Flask for the backend and a Decision Tree Classifier model, this web app provides instant disease predictions based on user-input symptoms.

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms in a simple, user-friendly interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier trained on a comprehensive dataset predicts potential diseases.
  3. Prediction: The model provides instant results, helping users understand possible health concerns.
  4. Additional Information: The app offers insights on the predicted disease, including symptoms, precautions, medications, and lifestyle tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: A sleek and intuitive design ensures seamless navigation.
  • Accurate Predictions: Leveraging AI and machine learning, the app delivers reliable disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Health Insights: Users gain access to relevant health information to make informed decisions.
  • Educational Content: The platform includes health-related blog posts to enhance awareness.

Conclusion

My goal is to make healthcare more accessible by integrating AI with medical knowledge. This project serves as a stepping stone toward that vision, enabling users to gain quick insights into potential health conditions.
